Frequently Asked Questions
What is ZA-DIVERGENT?
An open-source intelligence bulletin covering South Africa and the surrounding region. Signals are collected, scored, and published by the FORGE OSINT engine. All source material is publicly available.
Is all the content really free?
Yes. The full bulletin — all signals, cases, entity profiles, court records, map, and graph — is free with no account required. Pro will add automation features (API feeds, email digests, custom alerts) for power users when it launches.
When does Pro launch?
We're still defining which features belong in the Pro tier. Register your email above and we'll notify you when it's ready. No spam, just one announcement.
How can I support this project now?
Use the "Support Independent OSINT" button in the footer. Every contribution helps keep the bulletin running and the data pipeline active.
