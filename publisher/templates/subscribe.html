Frequently Asked Questions
What is ZA-DIVERGENT?
An open-source intelligence bulletin covering South Africa and the surrounding region. Signals are collected, scored, and published by the FORGE OSINT engine. All source material is publicly available.
Is the free tier permanent?
Yes. The public bulletin will always be free. Pro adds depth — full evidence chains, entity dossiers, and machine-readable feeds.
How are payments processed?
Payments are processed securely by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway licensed by the South African Reserve Bank. We never see or store your card details.
My card doesn't work?
Use the Manual EFT option above. Send proof of payment to the listed email for manual activation within 24 hours.
